Our Services

At Zok Internet Café, we provide professional digital services tailored to meet the unique needs of our Thokoza community. From government portal assistance to professional document preparation, we're here to help you navigate technology with confidence.

Services We Offer

Printing

Black and white and colour printing for documents, IDs, and photographs. Single pages and bulk orders welcome.

From R2.00 per page

Scanning

High quality document scanning to digital format. Ideal for archiving IDs, certificates and important paperwork.

From R5.00 per page

SARS e-Filing

Expert help with SARS e-Filing, including tax return submissions, compliance letters, and account queries.

From R50.00 per session

CV Typing

Professional CV typing and formatting to help you stand out. We also assist with cover letters and motivations.

From R50.00 per CV

PSIRA Registration

Assistance with PSIRA new registrations and annual renewals for security officers.

From R80.00 per application

Other Services

Varies by service
